ABOUT US

Music for us is emotion. Raw, untamed, intense, imperfect.

And the desire to run fingers through its raw edges, smoothening them in some parts and breaking it further in others. Using its intensity to express , deeply and wholly - That's what brought Kintsugi Studios about.

Kintsugi Studios is a creative music/audio and video production studio based in Chittranjan Park, New Delhi. We compose and produce music for Films, TV and Brands. We offer composition, recording, mixing and mastering services for bands, artists and agencies. We also provide creative video solutions for artists and brands.

Recently, we launched Kintsugi Sessions, a music performance video series. The first season has been successfully launched featuring some of the best musicians and bands from around the world and the country. These sessions not only capture a performance, but bring to you, the essence, the flavor and the experience of listening to these artists. Kintsugi Sessions were also featured in articles by Rolling Stone India, Mint Lounge and Wild City.

Kintsugi Studios was founded by Abhishek Sekhri in 2014.

An 'Engineering Physics' bachelor of technology graduate from Delhi College of Engineering by academic background, his passion for music dates back to over a decade. Largely he is a self-taught music aficionado. From playing the guitar to mixing music to short films to music videos, his work has evolved and has been recognized on many platforms.
